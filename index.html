About Me

Welcome to my blog! My name is Elliot Jamhuri Nkwama, and I’m a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast, software developer, and technology explorer. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Cyber Security at the Institute of Accountancy Arusha (IAA) with an average GPA of 4.36, I specialize in areas such as penetration testing, embedded systems, web development using Python, and machine learning. Through this blog, I share insights, tutorials, and experiences from my journey in the tech world. From building secure applications and exploring IoT innovations to conducting cutting-edge research in cybersecurity, my goal is to inspire, educate, and connect with like-minded tech enthusiasts. Feel free to explore my projects, share your thoughts, and join me on this exciting technological journey!
